# Offer Package Ideas (Adapted from Inspiration)

Purpose: productized offers with clear value and indicative pricing, aligned to your consulting/services strengths (grants, strategy, digital, AI, content).

1) AI Automation Sprint  
   - What: 2-week build of 1–2 automations (intake → email → tracking; doc summary; reporting).  
   - Value: saves manual ops time; includes SOPs and handoff.  
   - Indicative price: 500–1 500 $.

2) AI Ops Playbooks (clients)  
   - What: ready-to-use workflows/templates for client support or intake, with prompts and guardrails.  
   - Value: faster response, consistent quality, safer AI use.  
   - Price: 400–1 200 $.

3) Viral Landing + Content Kit  
   - What: landing page (headline/subheadline/CTA + 3 bullets), 10 post templates, and a 30-day schedule.  
   - Value: faster launch of offers; plug-and-play social posts.  
   - Price: 500–1 200 $.

4) Personal Brand Accelerator (consulting version)  
   - What: positioning, bio, 30-day content plan, 10 prompts, profile optimization FR/EN.  
   - Value: clearer authority + pipeline for calls.  
   - Price: 500–1 500 $.

5) Thursday Call Vault (office hours)  
   - What: weekly group call recording vault + monthly Q&A.  
   - Value: scalable support; clients get answers without 1:1.  
   - Price: 50–150 $/mo or 300–600 $/year.

6) Funnel Templates (services)
